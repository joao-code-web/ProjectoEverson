I have a degree in Chemistry from Ceará State University, Brazil, where I worked as a teaching assistant in Inorganic and Physical Chemistry and conducted research projects in Computational Chemistry in GQTE (Theoretical and Electrochemical Chemistry Group), using DFT to investigate the adsorption process on metallic surfaces.

Additionally, I received scholarships during the entire course due to my high academic performance and two bronze medals that I won in the Ceará Undergraduate Chemistry Olympiads in Organic and Inorganic Chemistry.

Nowadays, I work as a chemistry teacher for high school students who aim for the highly competitive entrance exam for Medicine. I also mentor a group of students learning to use the ORCA software.
