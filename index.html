I have a degree in Chemistry from the State University of Ceará, Brazil, where I worked as a teaching assistant in the fields of Inorganic and Physical Chemistry. I have also received awards in scientific Olympiads from this institution. Currently, my research involves analyzing how organic molecules can interact with metallic surfaces to prevent corrosion. Furthermore, I am teaching chemistry to high school students who are preparing for the highly competitive entrance exam for Medicine and mentoring a group of students who are learning to use the ORCA software. Apart from my academic and professional responsibilities, I find joy in sharing knowledge by creating free materials to assist other students in their studies."
