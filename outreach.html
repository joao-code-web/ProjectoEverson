Outreach

Founder of School of Computational Chemistry

The Computational Chemistry School aims to provide high school students with their first experience in scientific research and enable the development of the necessary skills for them to become future scientists. During the project, the students will act as researchers, where they will develop a project and receive guidance from graduate students. Additionally, they will learn Python programming, with a focus on the use of Artificial Intelligence. Alongside this, they will have the opportunity to enhance various important skills, such as reading scientific articles, English, and personal organization, through monthly workshops. The project's goal is to foster interest in scientific research from high school and prepare students for a future in academia or a professional career through a stimulating and challenging environment.
